Orbit

A personal life dashboard for iPhone, iPad, Mac, Apple TV & Apple Watch

Orbit pulls the different threads of everyday life — your calendar, tasks, health, money, people, and more — into one dashboard, with an on-device AI that files what you capture and answers questions about your own life. Not yet released; built with the same private, on-device philosophy as ClockRings.

← Back to Products

How it works

Jot anything into Capture — one box, text or voice — and Orbit's on-device AI cleans it up and files it as a task, note, idea, person, or quote. A Today dashboard surfaces what actually matters, and Ask Orbit is right there to answer questions using everything you've stored, all without your data ever leaving the device.

Your day, at any horizon

Step the same dashboard back through Day, Week, Month, or Year — the modules stay the same, just the window changes.

  • Briefing — a daily summary of what's ahead, pulled together automatically
  • Timeline — a single scrollable timeline of your day, pulled from Calendar, Health, Photos, and everything else you've logged
  • Focus — timed work sessions with a Live Activity on the Lock Screen
  • Routines — daily habits with streaks
  • Weather — today's forecast, right on the dashboard
  • Search — one search box across everything you own: people, projects, vehicles, trips, tasks, reminders, and notes

Calendar, reminders & inbox

  • Calendar & Reminders — your real Apple Calendar and Reminders, not a duplicate; pin any individual list or calendar as its own dashboard section
  • Email — an inbox glance over your own account
  • Deliveries — Orbit spots package trackers in your inbox and surfaces them automatically

Life admin, in one place

  • Projects — active work, retainer countdowns, and the tasks linked to each one
  • Content — a pipeline from idea to published
  • Work — a pay-cycle glance, upcoming holidays, and a leave balance — every field is yours to set
  • Retirement — a quick years-to-go / income-at-retirement glance, with a full forecast in the detail view
  • Garage — vehicles, fuel, service records, and maintenance alerts
  • Travel — trips, segments, and packing lists
  • Calculator — a quick tip calculator, plus a full calculator, right on the dashboard

Money

  • Stocks — your watchlist: price, change, and a sparkline
  • App Sales — your own App Store sales, stepping through Day / Week / Month / Year

People & memories

  • People — who to reconnect with, upcoming birthdays, and an inner circle you pin to the top
  • Library — notes, journal entries, and highlights, resurfaced "on this day"
  • Journal — a dedicated place to write, synced into your Library, with one tap out to Day One
  • Photos — recent shots, shared albums, and favorites, pulled straight from your own library

Everywhere you need it

  • iPhone & iPad — the full dashboard, a tab bar on iPhone and a sidebar on iPad
  • Mac — the same app, native, with a sidebar
  • Apple TV — a dashboard for the big screen
  • Apple Watch — a glanceable companion, right on your wrist
  • Home & Lock Screen widgets — the dashboard, at a glance, without opening the app
  • Siri, Shortcuts, Spotlight & CarPlay — capture a thought, ask Orbit, get your briefing, or check what's due, hands-free
  • Appearance — System, Light, or Dark, with the same accent-color picker as ClockRings
  • Lock — Face ID, Touch ID, Optic ID, or a passcode keeps the app private
  • Private iCloud sync — no account or login; everything stays in your own iCloud

Status
