See how ClockRings tracks time spent on individual operations during your shift — with automatic lunch-time removal, flexible reporting periods, and one-tap sharing of your totals.

  • Logging time on operations throughout your tour
  • Scope picker: Day, Week, Pay Period, Month, Year
  • Lunch time automatically netted out of totals
  • Per-operation breakdown plus grand total
  • Share as Image, Text, PDF, or CSV

ClockRings is a shift-tracking and pay-estimation app built for USPS and other shift workers — with decimal/standard time views, Live Activities punch tracking, widgets, and on-device pay period reporting. No accounts, no data uploads — everything stays private on your device.
